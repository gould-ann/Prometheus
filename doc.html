About Us/Where we Come In

Stronger together

In order to get around a lot of the problems with conventional data storage problems (prone to hacking, unable to correct errors or repair lost data) our servers utalize a somewhat newer form of data storage called blockchain. We maintain accsess to multiple servers which contain digital "blocks" of data. When one block is changed, it breaks the chain. And when one chain is broken, other servers are able to repair it. We also encrypt each bit of data with multiple levels of security. This ensures that only the people you totally trust can get to your personal identifiable information (PII). Storing our data together also means that we can find trends and stop diseases early. When we store our data together, trust eachother, and work together, the possibilities are endless.
